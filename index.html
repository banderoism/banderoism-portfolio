Services built for business outcomes

Every project is designed to do one thing: help more people understand you, trust you, and choose you.

Brand Story Video

A short, story-driven video that explains who you are, what you offer, and why customers should trust you. Perfect for websites, About pages, and pinned social posts.

  • Discovery call & simple strategy
  • Interview + b-roll filming (2–3 hours)
  • 60–90 second main video
  • 2 social cutdowns (15–20 seconds)
  • Two rounds of revisions

Typical projects: $1,200–$2,000

Promo & Campaign Videos

Product, service, or offer-focused videos built for campaigns, ads, and launches. Designed with strong hooks, clear messaging, and direct calls to action.

  • Concept development & script / shot list
  • Producer-led shoot day
  • 30–60 second hero video
  • 3–5 platform-specific versions
  • Music license & sound mix

Typical projects: $2,000–$5,000

Event Highlights

Recap videos that keep your event alive long after it ends and give you content for sponsors, socials, and future promotion.

  • 2–4 hour or half-day coverage

  • Two rounds of revisions

Typical projects: $800–$1,500

Aerial & Real Estate Video

About Frank

I’m Frank Fernandez, a creative producer working between Tijuana and San Diego. I help businesses, organizations, and creators turn ideas into clear, effective video content.

My background combines documentary instincts with commercial needs: real people, real stories, produced with a clean, professional finish.

On every project, I focus on three things:

  • Clarity of message
  • Smooth production for you and your team
  • Deliverables that actually support your business goals, not just views

Banderoism is my personal creative label; the work you see here is where that direction meets practical business results.
